SkipAd Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

SkipAd ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that we do not collect, use, or share any of your personal information because our app operates entirely on your device.

Core Privacy Principles

  • 100% Offline Operation: SkipAd never connects to the internet
  • Zero Data Collection: We never collect, save, or transmit any user data
  • Complete Local Processing: All functionality runs entirely on your Mac
  • No Analytics or Tracking: We have no way to know how you use the app

Information We Do NOT Collect

SkipAd is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect any information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Screen content or captured images
  • Browsing history or activity
  • Location data
  • Any other form of user data

Screen Recording Permission

SkipAd requires screen recording permission to function. This permission is used exclusively for:

  • Detecting skip buttons on your screen
  • All processing happens in real-time on your device
  • No screenshots or screen recordings are saved
  • No screen content leaves your device
  • Images are immediately discarded after processing

How SkipAd Works

SkipAd operates entirely offline and locally:

  • The app uses macOS's native frameworks for button detection
  • All processing is performed on your Mac's processor
  • No internet connection is required for the core functionality
  • The app does not communicate with any external servers

Subscription Information

If you choose to subscribe to SkipAd Pro:

  • All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • Apple may collect information as described in their privacy policy
  • Your subscription status is stored locally on your device

Data Storage

SkipAd stores minimal data locally on your device:

  • Your app preferences (detection interval, language settings)
  • Subscription status (free or pro)
  • Daily skip count (for free users)

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by removing the app.

Third-Party Services

SkipAd does not use any third-party services for:

  • Analytics or tracking
  • Advertising
  • Crash reporting
  • User behavior monitoring

Data Security

Since SkipAd does not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of your data being intercepted or breached. All functionality is contained within the app on your device.

Children's Privacy

SkipAd does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13, as it does not collect any information at all.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of SkipAd after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. If you want to remove all local data, simply delete the app from your Mac.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or SkipAd's privacy practices, please contact us through:

  • The App Store product page
  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/xjwhnxjwhn/SkipAd/issues
